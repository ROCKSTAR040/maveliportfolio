About Me

I was an Asura and a big devotee of Lord Vishnu. Still today, Iam considered as the most popular in the beautiful state of Kerala and was greatly respected in his kingdom as he was considered to be wise, judicious, and extremely generous.

I was the grandson of Prahlada and the son of Virochana.I was taught the Vedas by his grandfather Prahlada and later by his teacher Shukracharya.

Shukracharya was a very good teacher, hence with his help, I soon conquered the three worlds. Through this, I defeated Lord Indra, the King of the Gods, and took over the heavens.

During my rule rich and poor were equally treated. There was neither crime, nor corruption. People did not even lock their doors, as there were no thieves in my kingdom. There was no poverty, sorrow or disease in my reign and everybody was happy and content.

I was very generous and charitable. Whenever anybody approached me for help or requested for anything I always granted. To test the King, Lord Vishnu disguised himself as a dwarf and a poor Brahmin called Vamana. He came to the me, just after I performed my morning prayers and was preparing to grant boons to Brahmins.

Vamana asked for three feet of land and I has agreed. Vamana, grew to a gigantic figure, and measured the Earth using one foot, and the entire universe in his next. I realized that vamana was none other than beloved god and asked vamana to place his third foot on my head making myself to live in the patala, also called as Sutala Loka.
